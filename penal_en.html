Criminal Law Services

At López Márquez Abogados, we understand the importance of having solid and expert defense in criminal matters. Our team is prepared to provide high-quality legal advice and representation at every stage of the criminal process, from the initial investigation to defense in court. We are dedicated to protecting our clients' rights with a strategic and personalized approach, always seeking the best possible resolution for each case.

    • Comprehensive Criminal Defense: We offer full defense in criminal cases, ensuring expert legal representation at all stages of the process.

    • Preventive Advice: We guide our clients to minimize legal risks in their personal or business activities to prevent potential criminal conflicts.

    • Crimes Against Property and the Socioeconomic Order: Representation in cases of fraud, misappropriation, theft, and other related crimes.

    • Road Traffic Offenses: Advice and defense in cases related to traffic offenses and crimes.

    • Crimes Against Physical and Moral Integrity: Representation in cases of injuries, threats, coercion, mistreatment, and any crime affecting the physical and moral integrity of our clients.

    • Crimes Against Honor and Privacy: Protection and defense in cases of defamation, slander, and crimes affecting personal or family honor and privacy.

    • Cybercrime: Advice and defense in cases of cybercrimes, such as cyberbullying, online fraud, identity theft, and crimes against privacy on the internet.

Do you need legal advice? Feel free to contact us. Our team is ready to offer you an initial consultation at no cost and help you make the best decision.
