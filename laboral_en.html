Labor Law Services

We offer comprehensive services for workers and companies facing labor issues. We support our clients every step of the way, from initial advice to court representation, ensuring the best defense of their rights and protection of their interests. Below, we highlight the key areas where we can assist you:

    • Employment Contracts and Hiring Modalities

      - Drafting, reviewing, and modifying employment contracts.

      - Advice on different types of contracts and their implications (permanent, temporary, internship, part-time, etc.).

    • Dismissals and Compensation Claims

      - Management and advice on dismissal cases (objective, disciplinary, collective).

      - Compensation claims and calculation of severance payments.

    • Collective Bargaining and Agreements

      - Assistance in negotiating collective agreements and advising trade unions or worker representatives.

      - Resolution of collective labor disputes.

    • Wage and Labor Rights Claims

      - Advice on claiming unpaid wages.

      - Defense of labor rights, such as family reconciliation rights, equal treatment, and occupational health and safety.

    • Occupational Risk Prevention and Health at Work

      - Consultancy on safety measures and labor regulations.

      - Advice on medical leave processes, workplace accidents, and occupational diseases.

    • International Mobility and Labor Immigration Advice

      - Assistance with work permits and advice for foreign workers in Spain.

      - Management of visas and permits for companies wishing to hire foreign employees.

For more information or to discuss your case, do not hesitate to contact us. Our team will review your situation and get in touch with you to provide the best solution and guide you through every step of the process.
