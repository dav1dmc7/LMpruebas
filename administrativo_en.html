Administrative Law Services

We are ready to support you at every step and protect your rights against public administrations. Our firm offers services in the following areas:

    • Advisory on Licenses and Permits: We help manage and obtain municipal licenses, activity permits, and other necessary authorizations for the development of business or personal projects.

    • Administrative Appeals and Claims: We represent your interests in claims against administrative decisions, fines, sanctions, and arbitrary resolutions. We handle appeals and take your case to court if necessary.

    • Public Procurement: We advise businesses on all matters related to public procurement, including the preparation of tenders, bids, and resolving potential conflicts arising from contracts with public entities.

    • Expropriations: We accompany you through the expropriation process, ensuring your rights are respected and you receive fair compensation. We ensure compliance with legal requirements and protect your interests.

    • Liability Claims: In case of damages caused by errors or negligence from the administration, we manage claims so you can receive fair compensation.

    • Disciplinary Procedures: We offer defense and representation in disciplinary proceedings before the administration, protecting your rights at every stage of the process.

Do you have an administrative issue or need legal guidance? Contact us and let our team advise you to achieve effective and tailored solutions.
