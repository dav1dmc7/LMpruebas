About Us

Hello and welcome to López Márquez Abogados

We are a team of young and enthusiastic lawyers who firmly believe in bringing law closer to people in a simple, approachable, and friendly way. Our journey began when two passionate lawyers from Madrid and Barcelona decided to join forces to make a dream come true: to provide quality legal services to everyone.

What makes us different?

    • Passion for what we do: We love our profession, and this is reflected in the commitment and dedication we put into every case.

    • Approachability and empathy: We understand that the legal world can be overwhelming. That's why we strive to explain everything clearly and guide you through each step.

    • Innovation: We take advantage of technology to offer you an efficient and agile service, without losing the personal touch.

Our services cover various areas, such as labor law, immigration, civil, criminal, and administrative law.

We want you to feel that we are by your side, whether it's to advise you, defend your rights, or simply provide you with guidance when needed. We believe in building relationships based on trust and mutual respect.

Would you like to get to know us?

We invite you to explore our website, where you'll find more information about how we can support you, as well as our social media links, which you can find in our contact section. And if you have any questions or simply want to talk about your situation, don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to listen to you and work together toward the best solution.

At López Márquez Abogados, your peace of mind is our greatest reward. Let's team up and reach your goals together!

We look forward to meeting you, and remember that we will always strive for you to go in the right direction.
