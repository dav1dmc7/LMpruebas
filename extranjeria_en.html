Immigration Law Services

We offer comprehensive and personalized advisory to foreigners who wish to regularize their status in Spain. Our specialized immigration team provides guidance at every step, ensuring a safe process tailored to each need. Some of the services we offer include:

    • Personalized Situation Assessment: We evaluate each case individually to identify the most suitable path for regularization: social integration, labor integration, NIE, or family-based residence.

    • Document Processing and Management: We handle the collection and preparation of all necessary documentation for residence, NIE, or other permits, ensuring compliance with the Immigration Office’s requirements.

    • Guidance on Regularization Processes: We support clients in applying for residency through integration, helping them demonstrate social integration and meet the required criteria.

    • Services for Entrepreneurs and Professionals: For those wishing to work, start a business, or invest in Spain, we provide advice on obtaining the NIE, work permits, and the necessary processes to begin professional activities in the country.

    • Asylum and Refugee Services: We provide advisory services for international protection applications.

Additionally, our team will guide and advise you through any complications and/or potential rejections by presenting appeals and the necessary arguments.
