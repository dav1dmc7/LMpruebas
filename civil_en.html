    Civil Law Services

    We provide advisory and representation services in all aspects of civil law to protect your personal, family, and property rights and interests. Below are some of the key areas in which we can assist you:

      • Contracts and Obligations: Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating all types of contracts (sales, leases, donations, etc.), ensuring that you can sign with full confidence, knowing that your expectations are being met.

      • Family Law: We advise and support you during divorces, asset separations, child custody, alimony, adoptions, and inheritances. We guide you through each step to resolve family situations fairly and respectfully.

      • Successions and Inheritances: Creating and opening a will are very delicate matters. We guide you through the planning and distribution of inheritances, ensuring a smooth transition without conflicts, with the utmost respect and dedication.

      • Claims and Civil Liability: Advisory and representation in claims for damages and losses, whether in accident cases or contractual breaches. We defend your rights to obtain the compensation you deserve.
